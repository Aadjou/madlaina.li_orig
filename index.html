My name is Madlaina Kalunder and I'm a Game Designer. I graduated in 2014 from University of Arts in Zürich, Switzerland in the field of Game Design. I’m a fast-minded, creative problem solver that thinks outside the box. I enjoy working and interacting with people and I am not shy to approach difficulties. My strength is to keep a general view over complex projects. As an allrounder, I create game concepts, programming, 3D modelling and story telling.
